Simplification

A simplicication of an image, cover art or anything else is all about having
the most essential details, and leave the rest out. A viewer should just be able to know
what it is without a problem, concidering the viewer knows about the original design.
In my case I chose one of my favourite albums from the rapper Lil Durk and simplified the cover.
I chose to have his hair in there because it symbolizes him, toghether
with his iconic chain and a jaw line to get some structure.
